Space Jump

You've landed on an alien world in search of fortunes and adventure. Unfortunately, there's nothing but rocks and a thin atmosphere. Also, you're getting hungry.

It's time to go home.

But, there's a problem. There's too much debris in orbit. That, and the fact that your weak-sauce engine isn't anywhere near powerful enough to escape this planet's gravity.

Nevermind that, the fearless captain (you) always has a plan! Maybe if you can just get up to that first piece of floating space junk...
